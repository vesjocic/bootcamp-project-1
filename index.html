A little bit about us...

Loose Chain Bike Co. specializes in the needs of the downtown Toronto bike commuter. With our network of 16 repair shops, we do everything from spring tune-ups to emergency troubleshooting. We also host workshops and events aimed at empowering casual urban cyclists. You can read more about our story here.
